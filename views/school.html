User Trialling Rijksstudio

The purpose of this research was to test the quality of usability of the Rijksmuseum website, more specifically, the Rijksstudio part of it.

It was research concentrated on how a specific group of users (students, interested in art and museums)

interacts with the Rijksmuseum website and what difficulties

do they face according to our research questions.

Prototyping in Adobe XD

In school we had to make an application about ourself with a working interface.

After successfully completing this course I know the basics of Adobe XD and understand concepts of prototyping tools.

After completing this course, participants can:

navigate in Adobe XD -

build interactive prototypes for digital applications -

understand key concepts of prototyping applications -

e.g.,components,characterstyles,artboards,buttons - get an idea of problems and solutions within interface design
